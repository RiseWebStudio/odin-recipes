Omelette with tomatoes

Description

A tomato omelet is a simple and quick breakfast that can be prepared in a few minutes. This omelet has a fresh and juicy flavor thanks to the tomatoes, which add freshness and texture. You can serve it as a separate dish or with fried bread to make it more filling.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tomato
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Break the eggs into a bowl and beat them with a fork.
  2. Dice the tomato.
  3. Add salt, pepper and chopped tomato to the eggs. Stir to combine.
  4. Heat the oil in a medium skillet over medium heat.
  5. Add the egg mixture to the pan. Spread the tomatoes evenly.
  6. Move the egg mixture with a spatula during cooking to make it fluffy and golden.
  7. Transfer the cooked omelet to a plate and serve.
