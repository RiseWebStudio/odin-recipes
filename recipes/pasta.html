Pasta with chicken and mushrooms

Description

Chicken pasta is a simple and delicious recipe that can be prepared in a few minutes. This pasta has a rich and intense flavor thanks to the chicken and tomatoes that add freshness and texture. You can serve it as a separate dish or with hot bread to make it more filling and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams of spaghetti
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 200 grams of mushrooms
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of cream sauce

Steps

  1. Fill a large saucepan with water, add salt, and bring to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook over medium heat for 10-12 minutes or until the spaghetti is tender.
  2. Cut the chicken breasts and mushrooms into small cubes.
  3. Heat olive oil over medium heat in a medium skillet.
  4. Add the diced chicken breasts and mushrooms to the skillet and saut√© until golden brown.
  5. Add the garlic cloves, salt and pepper. Stir to combine.
  6. Add the cream sauce to the pan and mix thoroughly with the chicken and mushrooms.
  7. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan and stir to coat the spaghetti with the sauce.
  8. Serve the pasta on plates and garnish with chopped fresh parsley or Parmesan cheese, if desired.
